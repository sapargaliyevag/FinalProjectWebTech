Aida Kaumenova
Aida Kaumenova, a talented designer from Kazakhstan, has won recognition in different parts of the world. All Aida's collections are filled with Kazakh culture, which gives her works a special beauty and harmony. The fashion house of the charming Kazakh woman is known under the brand Aida KaumeNOVA. The products are popular in Kazakhstan and abroad. Aida Kaumenova, whose biography includes many victories in the professional sphere, is happy in her personal life and full of creative ideas.
